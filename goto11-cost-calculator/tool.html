Wasted Meeting Time: Fully loaded annual compensation divided by 2080 working hours then by 60 minutes multiplied by attendees and duration. Same methodology as Bain and Company, McKinsey, and Harvard Business Review.

Rework and Follow-up: Same hourly rate applied to rework headcount and combined rework time.

Delay and Opportunity Cost: Initiative value divided by 365 days multiplied by delay days.

Annual Projection: Per-incident cost times weekly frequency times direct reports times 52 weeks. Conservative mode applies a 50 percent waste factor.

Sources: Bain and Company Your Scarcest Resource HBR 2014. Atlassian Time Wasted in Meetings. Microsoft Work Trend Index 2023.
