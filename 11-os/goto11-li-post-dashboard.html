How to import LinkedIn metrics

Step 1: Go to LinkedIn Analytics (Me → Posts & Activity → Analytics).

Step 2: Click Export in the top right and pick your date range.

Step 3: Click Import LinkedIn CSV above and select the downloaded file.

Step 4: Review the matched and unmatched rows in the modal, then click Apply changes.

Tip: Posts that match by LinkedIn URL (saved in the expanded row) will sync most reliably. Add the URL after publishing for best results. Posts already linked to Notion will auto-sync the new metrics.

Suggested cadence: Run this every Friday to keep your performance data fresh.
