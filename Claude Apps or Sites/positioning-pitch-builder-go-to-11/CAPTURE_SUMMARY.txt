WHAT THE APP DOES
================================================================================

FUNCTION:
The Positioning Pitch Builder guides users through a structured workflow to
create, compare, and deploy positioning statements tailored to different
audience contexts.

WORKFLOW:
1. User authenticates via access form (name, email, company)
2. Selects target audience (6 categories: Tech, Finance, CPG, Services, Startup, Corporate)
3. Inputs positioning elements (Who, What, Problem, Solution)
4. Generates positioning statement variants
5. Compares and refines positioning
6. Saves/deploys to stakeholders

PRIMARY USE CASES:
- Sales pitch preparation
- Executive presentations
- Investor pitches
- Client engagement preparation
- Internal initiative approval

TARGET USERS:
- Go To 11 clients
- Go To 11 team members
- Leaders preparing to pitch

The app implements context-aware positioning by recognizing that different
audiences require different positioning approaches:

TECH/SAAS CONTEXT:
- Emphasizes innovation, integration, technical capabilities
- Target: Product managers, CTOs, engineering leaders
- Focus: ROI, efficiency, technical advancement

FINANCE CONTEXT:
- Emphasizes returns, risk mitigation, growth potential
- Target: Investors, financial managers, partners
- Focus: Capital efficiency, market opportunity, exit potential

CPG CONTEXT:
- Emphasizes consumer benefits, market fit, retail viability
- Target: Retailers, distributors, consumers
- Focus: Product-market fit, shelf appeal, demand

SERVICES CONTEXT:
- Emphasizes expertise, results, client outcomes
- Target: Procurement managers, CMOs, CFOs
- Focus: Capability, past results, ROI

STARTUP CONTEXT:
- Emphasizes market opportunity, team, defensibility
- Target: VCs, angels, board members
- Focus: TAM, team, competitive moat, traction

CORPORATE CONTEXT:
- Emphasizes strategic alignment, organizational benefit
- Target: Executives, decision makers
- Focus: Strategic fit, ROI, organizational transformation
