How a Go To 11 Proposal Gets Built

From client conversation to finished proposal deck — every step of the process.

Kenny
Claude
Collaborative
1
Kenny
Have the Client Conversation
Discovery call, intro meeting, or follow-up — learn what the client actually needs.
  • Listen for the real problem behind the ask
  • Note audience size, timeline, budget signals
  • Record with Granola, Fathom, or similar
2
Kenny
Grab the Transcript
Pull the transcript from your recording tool. Raw notes work too — Claude will sort through them.
  • Copy full transcript (no cleanup needed)
  • No recording? Jot notes on company, problem, scope, timeline, budget
3
Kenny
Paste into Cowork & Say "Build a Proposal"
One message kicks off the entire process.
  • "Build a proposal for [Company]. Here's the transcript: …"
  • Also: "draft a proposal," "put something together," "Gamma prompt for [Client]"
4
Claude
Extract Key Details
Claude reads the transcript and pulls out everything needed to build the proposal.
  • Company, industry, contact info
  • Core problem & success criteria
  • Audience size, level, format
  • Timeline, urgency, budget range
  • Competitive alternatives mentioned
5
Collaborative
Gap Analysis & Follow-Up Questions
Claude flags what's missing. You decide how to handle it.
  • Missing info? Claude drafts follow-up questions
  • Have the answers? Drop them in
  • Good enough? Say "go ahead"
6
Claude
Determine Tier & Calculate Pricing
Recommends a tier and builds the investment section using the pricing engine.
  • T1 Focused (~$7.5K)
  • T2 Growth (~$25K)
  • T3 Enterprise (~$60K)
Pricing Calculator
7
Claude
Assemble the Full Proposal
Up to 12 sections for formal proposals, 6–10 for discussion documents.
  • Opens with client's problem (never credentials)
  • Trust Multiplier embedded throughout
  • Three Pillars methodology applied
  • Kenny's voice — direct, warm, no fluff
8
Claude
Generate the Gamma Deck
Produces a Gamma-ready prompt for a designed, branded proposal deck.
  • Structured for Gamma's builder
  • Go To 11 brand colours applied
  • One click to generate at gamma.app
Paste into gamma.app
9
Kenny
Review, Adjust & Send
Review the deck, tweak, and send. Under 30 minutes total.
  • Check pricing, scope, tone
  • Adjust from your call instinct
  • Generate in Gamma, review design
  • Send with a warm follow-up note

Conversation → Proposal in Under 30 Minutes
